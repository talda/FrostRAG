<h2 id="character-and-monster-turns">Character and Monster Turns</h2><p>Every figure on the map — both characters and monsters — will get a turn each round. A figure’s turn starts when the previous figure ends their turn and concludes when the next figure begins theirs. During a figure’s turn, they will perform various actions and abilities based on their revealed ability cards.</p><p>Before we tackle the specifics of character and monster turns, we need to cover a variety of important concepts related to actions and abilities.</p>
