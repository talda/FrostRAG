<h3 id="loot-deck">Loot Deck</h3><blockquote>
<p><img alt="Frosthaven Identifier" class="fh-icon new-to-fh-icon" src="icons/general/fh-frosthaven-identifier-color-icon.png" title="Frosthaven Identifier"/> Each scenario will have a custom loot deck, built as directed by its scenario book entry and consisting of different types of loot cards. The number of cards of each type specified should be randomly drawn from their own individual decks and shuffled together to form the loot deck.</p>
<p>The four different types of loot are as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Money <img alt="Money Token" class="fh-icon" src="icons/general/fh-money-bw-icon.png" title="Money Token"/>:</strong> Twenty cards depicting one to three coins, which are each converted to gold based on the scenario level (see below).</li>
<li><strong>Material Resources:</strong> Eight cards per material type (lumber <img alt="Lumber Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-lumber-bw-icon.png" title="Lumber Icon"/>, metal <img alt="Metal Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-metal-bw-icon.png" title="Metal Icon"/>, hide <img alt="Hide Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-hide-bw-icon.png" title="Hide Icon"/>), which provide a varying number of materials based on the number of characters in the scenario.</li>
<li><strong>Herb Resources:</strong> Two cards per herb type (arrowvine <img alt="Arrowvine Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-arrowvine-bw-icon.png" title="Arrowvine Icon"/>, axenut <img alt="Axenut Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-axenut-bw-icon.png" title="Axenut Icon"/>, corpsecap <img alt="Corpsecap Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-corpsecap-bw-icon.png" title="Corpsecap Icon"/>, flamefruit <img alt="Flamefruit Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-flamefruit-bw-icon.png" title="Flamefruit Icon"/>, rockroot <img alt="Rockroot Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-rockroot-bw-icon.png" title="Rockroot Icon"/>, snowthistle <img alt="Snowthistle Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/loot/fh-snowthistle-bw-icon.png" title="Snowthistle Icon"/>), which provide one herb each.</li>
<li><strong>Random Item <img alt="Treasure Chest Icon" class="fh-icon" src="icons/general/fh-treasure-chest-bw-icon.png" title="Treasure Chest Icon"/>:</strong> One card that provides a random item (see <a href="#page_32">p.32</a>). This can only be gained once per scenario entry.</li>
</ul>
<p>Certain events will allow you to add a + sticker to a loot card, which provides one additional resource of that type.</p>
<p>Money and resources looted during a scenario are not gained until the end of the scenario (see <a href="#page_47">p.47</a>), but random items are gained immediately when looted.</p>
</blockquote>
