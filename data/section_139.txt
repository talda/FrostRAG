<h3 id="build">Build</h3><p><img alt="Build Icon" src="icons/general/fh-build-icon.png" title="Build Icon" width="42"/>  To build an unlocked building, the party must pay its build cost, which is shown on the building’s “L0” sticker (or printed directly on the map board itself in some cases) with resources from the Frosthaven supply.</p><p>When this cost is paid, cover it with the matching level 1 sticker and insert the matching level 1 card into the building deck in sequential order.</p><p>The party then gains the prosperity bonus and applies any other one-time effects indicated on the building card. During the next Outpost Phase, the building’s normal effect will resolve and its interactions will be available (unless it gets wrecked).</p>
