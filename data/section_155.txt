<h2 id="2-find-focus">2. Find Focus</h2><p>The monster finds an attack hex and focuses on an enemy with the following priority list:</p><ol>
<li>A hex with a movement path that triggers fewer negative hexes.</li>
<li>A hex with a movement path that requires fewer movement points.</li>
<li>An enemy closer by range.</li>
<li>An enemy earlier in the initiative order, following the normal rules for breaking tiesfor initiative (see <a href="#page_19">p.19</a>).</li>
