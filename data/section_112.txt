<h2 id="unlocking-new-classes">Unlocking New Classes</h2><p>New classes are unlocked through campaign progression. There are 11 new classes that can be unlocked. Each new class has two corresponding sealed tuck boxes, labeled with a class icon. When a class is unlocked, you are free to open its tuck boxes and inspect the contents, including the higher-level ability cards. That class is now available when a player creates a new character (see <a href="#page_65">p.65</a>).</p><table>
