<li><strong>Card Name:</strong> The name of the card.</li>
<li><strong>Initiative Value:</strong> This value determines when the character acts during the round (see <a href="#page_19">p.19</a>).</li>
<li><strong>Level:</strong> The level of the card. Level X cards are considered level 1 for all purposes.</li>
<li><strong>Actions:</strong> Each card has a top action and a bottom action. The abilities of an action are performed from top to bottom, with each ability separated by an ability line.
